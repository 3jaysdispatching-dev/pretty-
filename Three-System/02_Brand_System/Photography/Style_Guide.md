# Photography Style Guide

Photography should feel authentic, bright, and purposeful.

## Composition

- Favor clean, uncluttered frames.
- Use natural lines to direct attention toward the subject.
- Balance negative space with focal elements.

## Lighting and color

- Use soft, natural lighting when possible.
- Keep colors warm and harmonious with the brand palette.

## Subject matter

- Showcase real people, meaningful moments, and relevant product contexts.
- Use imagery that reinforces brand values like collaboration, innovation, and trust.

## Usage

- Pair photography with simple typography and generous whitespace.
- Avoid overly stylized filters or heavy visual effects.
