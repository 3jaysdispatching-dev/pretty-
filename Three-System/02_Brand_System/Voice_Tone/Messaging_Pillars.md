# Messaging Pillars

The brand voice should feel approachable, confident, and helpful.

## Pillars

- **Clear**: Use direct language and avoid jargon.
- **Warm**: Speak with a friendly, human tone.
- **Helpful**: Focus on user needs and success.
- **Confident**: Communicate trust and expertise.

## Application

- Use positive framing to guide users.
- Keep sentences concise and action-oriented.
- Avoid overly casual slang unless the brand calls for it.
