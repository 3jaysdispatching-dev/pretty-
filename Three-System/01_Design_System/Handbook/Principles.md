# Principles

Design and build for clarity, accessibility, and consistency.

## Clarity

Every interface should communicate purpose clearly. Use whitespace, hierarchy, and plain language.

## Consistency

Apply the same patterns, spacing, and visual language across components to make experiences predictable.

## Accessibility

Design for all users by meeting WCAG standards and supporting keyboard, screen reader, and reduced-motion preferences.

## Scalability

Build systems that grow with new products and platforms. Favor reusable tokens, components, and documented patterns.

## Craftsmanship

Pay attention to details, motion, and microinteraction quality. Small refinements create confidence and delight.
